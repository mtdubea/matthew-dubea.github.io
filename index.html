Welcome

This site presents my 2025 Mechanical Engineering Senior project: design of a front-wheel direct pedal powered tricycle manufactored to win the 2025 Acadiana 500 Tricycle competition in New Iberia, Louisiana. The design was custom fabricated using ULL resources, TIG welding, precision CNC machining using UL's Lathe, and FEA-backed structural analysis using Solidworks.
