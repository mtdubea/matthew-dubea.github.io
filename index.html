📚 Race Constraints & Requirements

  • Direct pedal–to–wheel drive only (no gears / chains).
  • Human-powered only — no motors.
  • Seat height ≤ 25 in; width ≤ 20 in.
  • Relay transitions in 30-ft zones.
  • 10-second penalty per violation.
