Project Overview

The Fast Pedal Engineers tricycle is a front-wheel, direct-pedal-drive racing vehicle designed for the 2025 Acadiana 500. Our goal was to build the lightest, safest, and fastest trike under a rule set that bans chains, gears, and motors.

  • Frame: recycled 1020-steel core + TIG-welded 4130 sub-frame
  • Rear axle: heat-treated 4140 shaft with custom brass bushings
  • Analysis: FEA predicted a 4.5 FoS under 800 N rider load
  • Total mass: 35 lb (15.9 kg)
  • Budget: $120 all-in
